MUT has outgrown spreadsheets. Your business needs real-time financial visibility that Google Sheets can't provide.

Real-Time Visibility
See instantly how prospective projects will impact your bottom line over the next 3, 6, or 12 months. No more manual updates or outdated projections.
Collaborative Workflows

Project-to-Revenue Tracking

The Solution

A custom financial platform built specifically for MUT's project-based creative business model.

Smart Project Management
Create budgets for potential projects, track stages from pitch to completion, convert approved budgets into income schedules automatically, and monitor actual vs projected costs in real-time.
Financial Intelligence
See how pending projects impact cash flow over the next 6-12 months. Compare projected vs actual revenue and expenses. Analyze profitability by project, client, or service type with customized dashboards by role.
Area-Based Budgeting
Each department maintains their annual budget independently. Track expenses against budget by area with approval workflows for changes and shared visibility with appropriate permissions.
Recurring & One-Time Transactions
Equipment rental income, rent and salaries, subscriptions, project-based income with flexible payment schedules, and one-time costs like equipment purchases all flow into one coherent view.

  • Project workflows from proposals to execution to payments
  • Equipment rental business models
  • Multi-month project timelines with variable payment schedules
  • The need to forecast "what if we land these projects?"
  • Area-based budgeting for collaborative creative teams
